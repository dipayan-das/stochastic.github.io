My Projects
• The Maze solving Grabber Robot   
  • Under guidance of Prof. Nabanita DasGupta
    Project Partners - Debajit De, Subhra Kamal Sengupta, Arnab Sarkar
The goal of our project was to develop a fully automatic system, which will be able to grab object and transport it from its start location to end location through maze.The main challenge was to navigate through mazes all by itself, by having a knowledge of the maze or by solving the maze 1st time with much amount of time, and other major challenge was to differentiate between a moveable object and a wall.Details.The description is outdated but still gives some idea about the working and building of the robot.The robot has gone through several upgrade after that.

The project was selected for ACEEE International Conference on “Recent Trends in Communication and Computer Networks 2013” (ComNet 2013)
